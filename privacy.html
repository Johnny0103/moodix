Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

What Moodix Does

Moodix is a static website prototype that helps users answer mood-check questions, import playlist information, and receive mood-based music recommendations.

Information You Provide

Moodix may ask for your name, email address, mood-check answers, selected music source, and playlist track information. In this prototype, this information is stored in your browser using localStorage unless you explicitly connect a third-party service.

Google and YouTube Data

If you connect YouTube, Moodix requests permission to read your YouTube playlists using the YouTube Data API. Moodix uses this access only to display your playlists, let you choose a playlist, import track titles and channel/artist information, and create mood-based song recommendations.

Moodix does not sell Google user data. Moodix does not use Google user data for advertising. Moodix does not transfer Google user data to third parties except as necessary to provide the user-facing playlist import and recommendation feature you request.

Google API Limited Use

Moodix's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Storage and Retention

Imported tracks, mood answers, and prototype sign-in details are stored locally in your browser. You can clear this data by clearing your browser site data for johnny0103.github.io.

Third-Party Services

Moodix may offer prototype connection paths for YouTube, Last.fm, Spotify, or other music sources. Each third-party service has its own privacy terms and authorization process.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the Moodix developer through the email listed in the Google Cloud OAuth consent screen.
